# 🚀 Jaxon's Mission

> A space-themed potty-training game built just for Jaxon — complete missions, earn gems, and unlock badges with your galactic co-pilot **Cosmo**! 👽✨

## 🌌 What Is This?

Jaxon's Mission is a gamified potty-training app dressed up as a space adventure. Jaxon earns gems for completing pee and poop missions, solves bonus math challenges for extra diamonds, and watches his mascot Cosmo react in real time. Streaks, achievements, check-in alerts, and a full stats lab keep the motivation going.

---

## 👽 Meet Cosmo

Cosmo is Jaxon's glowing alien co-pilot with an antenna and a breathing animation. He has four moods:

| Mood | When |
|---|---|
| 😄 Happy (jumping) | After a successful mission |
| 😟 Worried | When it's been too long between missions |
| 😲 Shocked | Emergency check-in alert |
| ⭐ Star-powered | Streak or achievement unlocked |

## 💎 Missions & Rewards

- 💦 **Pee Mission** → +1 Gem
- 💩 **Poop Mission** → +2 Gems
- ➕ **Math Bonus** → +1–3 Diamonds based on speed and performance
- 🏆 **Achievements** — unlockable badges like "First Mission" and "7-Day Streak"

## ✨ Features

- 👽 Cosmo mascot with real-time mood animations
- 🔔 Interval-based check-in alerts (configurable)
- 🧠 Adaptive math engine — Easy, Medium, and Genius+ difficulty
- 📊 Stats lab with pee/poop history graphs and streak tracking
- ⭐ Achievement badge system
- 📱 PWA — installable on any device
- 🌟 Neon space aesthetic with hot pink palette and cursor trail
- 📷 QR code for quick sharing
- 🔊 Voice toggle for audio feedback
